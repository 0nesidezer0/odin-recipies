Tacos

Description

These restaurant-worthy birria tacos are crispy on the outside, yet incredibly tender and juicy on the inside? What's not to love about this top-rated tacos de birria recipe?

Ingredients

  • Dried chile peppers
  • Beef
  • Seasoning
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Vinegar
  • Tortillas
  • Onion and Cilantro

Directions

  1. Boil the Dried Pappers for 5 Minutes
  2. Season the beef and sear on all sides in oil
  3. Blend the soaked chili peppers with reserved chile water
  4. Strain the sauce and pour over the meat. Cover the dutch oven
  5. Warm the tortillas on the griddle. Fill each tortilla with meat, onions and cilantro
